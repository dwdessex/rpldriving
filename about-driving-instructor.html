About John Folan, The Driving Instructor

A Peugeot 208 is used for driving lessons. The car has a manual gearbox, with air conditioning and has good visibilty making it easy to drive. The car is also fitted with dual controls for your safety.

Below is a quick look at what to expect from driving lessons with John Folan, the DSA Approved Driving Instructor who runs the Jolan Driving School.

  • Professional, friendly and patient approach.
  • Tips and advice on theory test.
  • Encouragement and support thoughout all your driving lessons.
  • Good prices and discounts on block bookings.
  • Punctual and reliable.
  • You can be picked up from wherever is best for you. This can be from work, home or school.
  • Flexible times so you can fit your driving lessons around you.
  • Once you've passed your driving test, a Pass Plus course can be taken.
  • Not driven for a while, or need to brush up on your skills? Refresher lessons will be ideal for you.
  • Honest appraisal of your progress.
  • Signed up to the DSA's voluntary code of practice for Approved Driving Instructors.
