Taking your first driving lesson in a manual car can be a daunting experience, but it can also be an exciting and rewarding one. It can be life changing, giving you the confidence and freedom to travel and get around West London and further afield.

As a special offer, you can get your first 90 minutes at the discounted price of £16. This gives you a chance to find out more about learning to drive, and to see how I teach.

My aim as a Driving Instructor is to teach you how to drive a manual car and become a safe driver for life. You will learn all the necessary skills and rules of the road.

I have a friendly, patient and professional approach to anyone wishing to learn how to drive. I will give you encouragement and support from your first lesson until the day you pass your driving test.   

Your success is as important to me as it is to you. So please take a look at my website and if you have any questions please don't hesitate to call or email me.

Most of all you want to enjoy it. I look forward to hearing from you.
